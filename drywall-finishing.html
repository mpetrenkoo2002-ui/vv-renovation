Drywall Finishing Services

Drywall finishing is what gives walls and ceilings their clean, seamless final appearance. VV Renovation provides precise drywall installation and finishing with attention to straight lines, smooth surfaces and ready-to-paint results.

We work with homeowners, designers and builders across the Greater Vancouver Area (Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Surrey, New Westminster, Coquitlam, Port Coquitlam, Port Moody, Maple Ridge, Pitt Meadows, White Rock, North Vancouver (City), North Vancouver (District), West Vancouver, Delta, Langley (City), Langley (Township)) to deliver clean, reliable and professional drywall finishing.

Types of Drywall Finishing We Offer

Drywall Installation

Accurate drywall board installation for walls and ceilings with proper alignment and clean preparation for finishing.

Taping & Mudding

Professional taping and mudding to reinforce joints, hide seams and create a solid base for a smooth final surface.

Skim Coating

Thin finishing coats that help create a refined, even and consistent wall surface ready for paint or final decoration.

Drywall Level 5 Finish

Premium level 5 finishing for ultra-smooth walls and ceilings, ideal for modern interiors and strong lighting conditions.

Ceiling Finishing

Clean ceiling finishing with careful detail work for flat, uniform surfaces and polished final results.

Repairs & Patches

Reliable drywall repair for damaged surfaces, holes, cracks and renovation touch-ups that blend neatly into the space.

How It Works

1

Consultation

We review your project, discuss the scope of drywall work and recommend the right finishing level for your space.

2

Preparation

We prepare the surfaces, install drywall where needed and make sure joints, corners and transitions are ready for finishing.

3

Finishing

Our team completes taping, mudding, sanding and skim coating with attention to smoothness, consistency and clean detail.

4

Final Review

We inspect the completed surfaces, refine final details and leave the space clean and ready for painting or the next stage.
