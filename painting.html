How It Works

1

Consultation

We review your space, discuss your goals, help with color direction if needed, and prepare a clear estimate for the project.

2

Preparation

We protect surrounding areas and prepare surfaces through patching, sanding, caulking, and priming where required.

3

Painting

Our team applies paint carefully and evenly for solid coverage, crisp edges, and a consistent professional finish.
