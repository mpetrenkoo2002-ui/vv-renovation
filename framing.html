Framing Services

Good framing is the foundation of a clean, durable and well-built interior. VV Renovation provides precise framing work for partitions, ceilings, renovation layouts and finish carpentry with attention to structure, alignment and long-term reliability.

We work with homeowners, designers and builders across the Greater Vancouver Area (Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Surrey, New Westminster, Coquitlam, Port Coquitlam, Port Moody, Maple Ridge, Pitt Meadows, White Rock, North Vancouver (City), North Vancouver (District), West Vancouver, Delta, Langley (City), Langley (Township)) to deliver clean, reliable and professional framing services.

Types of Framing We Offer

Steel Stud Framing

Precise steel stud framing for commercial and residential interiors, including partitions, bulkheads and layout changes.

Wood Framing

Strong and accurate wood framing for renovations, new interior structures and custom construction details.

Interior Partition Walls

Clean framing for new room layouts, basement divisions, office partitions and functional interior reconfiguration.

Ceiling & Bulkhead Framing

Framing for dropped ceilings, soffits and bulkheads with careful alignment and preparation for finishing work.

Finish Carpentry

Detail-oriented finish carpentry that completes the space with clean lines, accurate fitting and a refined final look.

Renovation Framing

Reliable framing solutions for remodels, additions and interior upgrades that need solid structure and clean execution.

How It Works

1

Consultation

2

3

Framing Installation

Final Review
