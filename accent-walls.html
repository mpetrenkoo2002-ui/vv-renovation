Accent Walls & Decorative Finishes

Accent walls are one of the most effective ways to transform a space without a full renovation. VV Renovation specializes in decorative feature walls including microcement, marble finishes, stucco textures, and modern wall designs.

We work with homeowners, designers and builders across the Greater Vancouver Area (Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Surrey, New Westminster, Coquitlam, Port Coquitlam, Port Moody, Maple Ridge, Pitt Meadows, White Rock, North Vancouver (City), North Vancouver (District), West Vancouver, Delta, Langley (City), Langley (Township)) to create unique interior spaces that elevate modern homes and commercial interiors.
