Flooring Services

Quality flooring changes the entire feel of a space. VV Renovation installs flooring with attention to preparation, leveling, clean layout and long-lasting results for homes and commercial interiors.

We work with homeowners, designers and builders across the Greater Vancouver Area (Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Surrey, New Westminster, Coquitlam, Port Coquitlam, Port Moody, Maple Ridge, Pitt Meadows, White Rock, North Vancouver (City), North Vancouver (District), West Vancouver, Delta, Langley (City), Langley (Township)) to deliver clean, reliable and professional flooring installation.

Types of Flooring We Install

Self Leveling

Smooth and even surface preparation for high-quality flooring installation and long-term durability.

Micro Cement Floor

Seamless and modern flooring with a clean look that works beautifully in contemporary interiors.

Hardwood

Classic hardwood flooring installation that adds warmth, value and timeless character to your space.

Engineered Wood

Vinyl

Practical and versatile vinyl flooring installation for residential and commercial environments.

1

Consultation

3

Installation

Final Finishing
