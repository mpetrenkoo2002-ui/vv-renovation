Tile & Stone Services

Tile and stone are some of the most durable and visually impactful finishes for modern interiors. VV Renovation installs tile and stone surfaces with precision, creating clean, long-lasting results for residential and commercial spaces.

We work with homeowners, designers and builders across the Greater Vancouver Area (Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Surrey, New Westminster, Coquitlam, Port Coquitlam, Port Moody, Maple Ridge, Pitt Meadows, White Rock, North Vancouver (City), North Vancouver (District), West Vancouver, Delta, Langley (City), Langley (Township)) to deliver high-quality tile and stone installation tailored to each project.

How It Works

1

Consultation

We discuss your project, materials, layout, design goals and recommend the right tile or stone solution.

2

Surface Preparation

Our team prepares the substrate carefully to ensure proper leveling, adhesion and long-term durability.

3

Installation

We install tile or stone with precision, paying close attention to alignment, spacing, cuts and finishing details.

4

Grouting & Final Finishing

We complete grouting, sealing where required, and leave the finished space clean, polished and ready to use.
